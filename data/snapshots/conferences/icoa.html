About ICOA

The 12th edition of the International Conference on Optimization and Applications - ICOA2026 provides a high-level international forum and an excellent venue for scientists, researchers, academic faculties, students, industry leaders, and others in the fields of optimization to present, discuss and publish their recent research results and approaches. The conference is an opportunity to develop new ideas and collaborations, to be aware of the latest search trends in the optimization techniques and their applications in the various fields. This 12th edition is enriched by very important special sessions that deal with problems using the latest methods of optimization.

IEEE Conference Record : #71047. All accepted and presented articles of ICOA2026 will be published in IEEE Xplore Digital Library indexed by Scopus

Supported by

Objectives

Expanding the concept of Optimization through recent research, with applications spanning multiple fields such as Mathematics, Computer Science Engineering, Telecommunications Engineering, Electrical Engineering, Mechanical Engineering, Industrial Engineering, Civil Engineering, Finance, Biomathematics, Healthcare Science, Economics, and Management. This exploration leverages a wide range of algorithms and methods to address diverse challenges.

Meetings

This conference provides a platform for researchers, industry leaders, innovators, professionals, and practitioners to explore the latest advancements in Optimization and simulation. Attendees will gain insights into cutting-edge numerical methods and the application of mathematical models across various fields.

On schedule

Lectures delivered by industry professionals and tutorials focused on specialized topics in Optimization. .
